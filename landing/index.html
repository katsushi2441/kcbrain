Try before you pay — two ways

We built verification in, because "trust me" is not a sales pitch.

1. The free browser Workbench

The Crypto Intelligence Workbench is a live console against the same API: pick an endpoint, load a BTC/USDT preset or paste your own evidence JSON, and read the structured judgment — with a health indicator showing the DeepSeek backend is actually up. Judge the quality yourself before spending a cent.

2. Verify the x402 machinery without paying

Every paid endpoint answers unpaid requests with a signed 402 challenge — so you can confirm the exact price, wallet, and network before any money moves:

curl -X POST https://bittensorman.xyz/kcbrain/decide/trade
# → HTTP 402: {"maxAmountRequired":"1000", "payTo":"0x444f…", "network":"base", …}

The full catalog is machine-readable at https://bittensorman.xyz/.well-known/x402.json — 24 paths under /kcbrain/. Also on Bankr (single-call skills only, flat $0.001).
